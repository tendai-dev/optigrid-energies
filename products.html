What We Offer

Product Categories

Inverters

Grid-tied, hybrid, and off-grid inverters from Victron, Solis, Fronius, Megarevo, and DEYE. Sizes from 3kW to 100kW+.

Lithium Batteries

High-performance lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries from BYD and Pylontech. 5kWh to 100kWh+ capacities.

Solar Panels

Tier-1 monocrystalline solar panels from Jinko and JA Solar. 450W to 550W modules with 25-year warranties.

Delta VSD-powered solar pumping systems for boreholes, irrigation, and water supply. No battery required.

Mounting Systems

Roof-mount, ground-mount, and carport structures engineered for Zimbabwe's climate conditions.

Monitoring Systems

Real-time monitoring and remote management platforms. Track your system's performance from anywhere.
