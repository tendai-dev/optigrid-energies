About OptiGrid Energy

Powering Zimbabwe's
Sustainable Future

We are Zimbabwe's leading solar energy company, committed to delivering clean, reliable, and affordable power solutions for homes, businesses, and communities.

Our Story

Built on a Passion for Clean Energy

OptiGrid Energy was founded in 2018 in Harare, Zimbabwe, by a team of engineers and energy enthusiasts who saw a better way to power the nation. Frustrated by unreliable electricity and rising costs, we set out to make solar energy accessible to every Zimbabwean household and business.

What started as a small operation installing rooftop panels for neighbours quickly grew into one of Zimbabwe's most trusted solar contractors. Today, we have completed over 1,000 installations across the country — from residential homes in Harare's suburbs to large commercial facilities in Bulawayo, Mutare, and beyond.

Our growth has been driven by a simple philosophy: deliver exceptional quality, transparent pricing, and unwavering after-sales support. Every system we install is designed to last decades, giving our clients true energy independence and peace of mind.

Our Mission

To accelerate Zimbabwe's transition to clean energy by providing world-class solar solutions that are accessible, affordable, and reliable.

Our Vision

A Zimbabwe where every home, business, and community is powered by clean, sustainable solar energy — reducing dependence on the grid and fostering energy independence.
