FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about going solar? Find quick answers below or reach out to our team.

How do I get a free solar quote?

Simply fill out the contact form above or call us directly. Our team will assess your property's energy needs, recommend the best system, and provide a detailed quote — all at no cost.

What areas in Zimbabwe do you serve?

We serve all major cities and towns across Zimbabwe including Harare, Bulawayo, Mutare, Gweru, Masvingo, Chinhoyi, Kwekwe, Marondera, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm availability in your location.

How long does it take to get a quote?

We typically respond to quote requests within 24 hours. After an initial consultation, we can provide a detailed proposal within 2-3 business days.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! In addition to our Pay-As-You-Go programme, we can connect you with financing partners who offer competitive solar loans. Visit our Services page for more details on PAYG options.

What warranties do you offer?

All our installations come with a workmanship warranty. Additionally, the equipment we install carries manufacturer warranties — typically 10-15 years for inverters, 10 years for batteries, and 25 years for solar panels.
